What are dogs?

The dog or domestic dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf which is characterized by an upturning tail.

Clifford

Clifford the Big Red Dog is an American children's book series about a big red dog named Clifford. Along with the children's book, a movie was released for it in 2021. Clifford the Big Red Dog is a 2021 adventure comedy film directed by Walt Becker and based on the children's book series of the same name by Norman Bridwell. It stars Jack Whitehall, Darby Camp, Tony Hale, Sienna Guillory, David Alan Grier, Russell Wong, Kenan Thompson, and John Cleese.

Characters

  • Clifford

    • A 2-year-old male red dog whose appearance, disposition and behavior are based on a giant dog. Clifford's pet owner is Emily Elizabeth. Clifford has a mother, two brothers, and two sisters, all of whom are normal-sized dogs. Clifford was originally the runt of the litter, seemingly fated to be small and sick, but grew to an enormous size apparently due to Emily Elizabeth's love and care.

  • Emily Elizabeth

    • Clifford's owner and human friend, an 8-year-old girl.[5] She is frequently portrayed riding him like a horse.
